System Engineering Project 2

What is it about?

This project involves innovating the Evebot Food Print Pen for the healthcare domain, specifically focusing on hospital food preparation kitchens. Currently, these kitchens rely on hard copy order chits for preparing food for patients. Our innovation aims to digitize and streamline this process by developing an automatic print pen conveyor system that prints food-safe ink orders directly onto serving trays instead of using order chits. This not only reduces the carbon footprint of the kitchen but also digitizes their existing processes.

  • Domain Research: Conducted extensive research into hospital food preparation processes to identify pain points and opportunities for digitization.

  • System Design: Developed a comprehensive system design that integrates the Evebot Print Pen with a conveyor system to automate the printing process.

  • Project Management: Coordinated with team members to ensure seamless communication and collaboration, utilizing project management tools to track progress and milestones.
