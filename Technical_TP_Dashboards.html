With Thingworx, I designed proof-of-concept dashboards for manufacturing and transport industries. These dashboards are invaluable for fleet monitoring and data-driven decisions.

My work demonstrates an ability to utilize cutting-edge technology, presenting complex data simply to enhance performance and efficiency.
