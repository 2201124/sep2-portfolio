Projects

System Engineering Project

The objective of our project was to develop a system that transforms food preparation, emphasizing enhanced presentation, customization, and efficiency while addressing the diverse needs of stakeholders, including restaurants, caterers, and consumers.


Formal Systems Engineering Processes (ISO/IEC/IEEE 15288) was employed to design a prototype, meticulously factoring in the unique requirements of the healthcare sector, such as nutritional needs and food safety. This resulted in a revolutionary system that not only exceeded expectations in food presentation and customization but also streamlined preparation processes.


Through this endeavor, It not only enhanced my problem-solving and project management capabilities but also acquired invaluable insights into the intricacies of healthcare systems engineering, strengthening my communication skills with diverse stakeholders.
