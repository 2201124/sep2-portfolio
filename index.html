Technical Projects

ROS Autonomous Waypoint Navigator


The challenge involved was to developing a solution that enables the AgileX Limo robot to navigate around the environment autonomously.

To achieve this, I familiarized myself with various navigation stack concepts, including GMapping, Global Planner, Local Planner, and Recovery Behaviors.

Using GMapping to map the environment, and subscribing to essential topic to send waypoint goals.

It resulted in the successful demonstration of the AgileX Limo's autonomous movement capability. This experience enhanced my ability to set up and use the ROS navigation stack on robotic devices.



ROS2 Box Folding Automation


The challenge was to introduce robotics and automation to improve a kitting assembly line. The kitting process analyzed and the decision was made to create a box folding jig and use a Cobot arm to automate it.

To achieve this, I familiarized myself with the necessary concepts, including robotic jig design and Cobot arm programming. I designed the box folding jig to ensure precise folds and programmed the Cobot arm to handle the box folding process efficiently.

This resulted in a successful demonstration of the automated box folding process and this experience enhanced my ability to integrate robotics and automation into manufacturing processes.
