IIOT Dashboarding Experience

What is it about?

My work with Thingworx involved creating IIOT dashboards for manufacturing and transport industries. These dashboards facilitate fleet monitoring and data-driven decision-making by providing critical insights through real-time data visualization.

  • Advanced Data Integration: Integrated diverse data sources into Thingworx via Kepware to provide a unified view, enabling real-time monitoring and decision-making.

  • Custom Visualization Development: Created custom widgets and visualizations in Thingworx to address specific user requirements and improve operational insights.

  • End-to-End Solution Delivery: Experienced the full lifecycle of dashboard development, from requirements gathering and design to implementation, testing, and deployment.
